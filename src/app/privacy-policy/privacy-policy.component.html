Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 2025

At Paintle.net, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the limited data we collect. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how you can control it.

Information We Collect

When you sign in with Google, we collect and store the following information:

  • Your Google ID (a unique identifier, not your email)
  • Your username
  • Your friend connections (e.g., who you've added)
  • Your player statistics and game progress

We do not collect your email address, password, profile picture, or any sensitive personal data.

How We Use Your Information

We use your data solely to:

  • Enable login and identify your account
  • Display your username and stats in the game
  • Let you view and connect with friends
  • Save your progress across devices

We do not share your data with third parties.

Your Data, Your Control

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by visiting your Account page and clicking the Delete Account button.

Once deleted, your data will be permanently removed from our database.
